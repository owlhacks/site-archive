Temple University's Hackathon

April 10th - 12th, 2015

Learn. Collaborate. Build.

Owlhacks is a premier hackathon in Philadelphia organized by students from Temple University. In April, 500 students will come to Philadelphia to learn, collaborate and build over the course of 36 hours. We recognize that hacking is about camraderie and technology, and we aim to facilitate the spirit of community inherent in #hackerculture.
FAQ What You Need To Know
When is Owlhacks? Owlhacks will begin on the evening of Friday, April 10th at 8pm and will end with the at 4pm on April 12th.
What's the schedule? Owlhacks follows a pretty typical 36-hours hour hackathon schedule. You can checkout the official schedule for specifics: Official Schedule
Who participates in Owlhacks? All the awesomest student developers under the sun! Owlhacks is open for any student to attend - beginners included. If you're a student, make sure you register.
Why come to Owlhacks? Owlhacks is an opportunity to grow as a creator, not just a developer. You'll be surrounded by some of the most creative and analytical minds in the country. Some of the most cutting-edge tchnologies will be on display, and by the end of the weekend, you could even win an award.
Where is Owlhacks? Owlhacks will be held in Temple University's Science, Education & Research Center (details).
How do I get in? The first step to getting into Owlhacks is applying. When you register, you submit a bunch of information about yourself that helps us decide if you'd be a good fit for Owlhacks. After we make a decision, we'll send you an email with the results.
Do Temple University students have to apply? Nope! We will have internal applications just for Temple University students. Keep watching your inbox for more information.
How do I get involved if I'm not a student? Only students can be hackers, but advisors are always handy. Shoot us an email if you're interested.
Will there be travel reimbursement? The short answer is no. We will not be compensating your travel to Owlhacks in most cases, BUT we're sending buses to some major hacker hubs in the region. Make sure you checkout our bus schedule. You can reserve a spot as soon as registration opens! Bus Info
How much does Owlhacks cost? Nothing! You don't need money to come to Owlhacks, just the willingness to learn and enthusiasm to create. You'll get free food and swag throughout the weekend - all you have to do is show up.
Who do I talk to if I have any questions? If you have any questions or comments at all, don't hesistate to send an email to team@owlhacks.com
