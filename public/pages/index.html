owlhacks

Temple University's Hackathon.

April 10th - 12th, 2015.

Learn. Collaborate. Build.

Owlhacks is a premier hackathon in Philadelphia organized by students from Temple University. In April, 500 students will come to Philadelphia to learn, collaborate and build over the course of 36 hours. We recognize that hacking is about camraderie and technology, and we aim to facilitate the spirit of community inherent in #hackerculture.
Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is Owlhacks?
    • Owlhacks is a hackathon hosted by Temple University students where we invite student programmers from all over to come to Philadelphia and show of their hacking skills. Upon arriving you can work in a group of up to four people for thirty-six hours to create a mind-blowing application, learn new technologies, or show-off by teaching others.
  • When is Owlhacks?
    • Owlhacks will begin the evening of Friday, April 10th, 2015 and will end with the prize ceremony on April 12th.
  • If i'm not a student, how can I help?
    • Unfortunately, if you are not a student you are not allowed to be on site during the hacking. However, if you are a skilled programmer and would be willing to mentor students during the course of the event, please reach out to us at yurkaninryan@gmail.com. Otherwise, feel free to see the awesome things students come up with during the demos on April 12th.
  • What do I need to bring?
    • You should bring a Laptop with a charger, a cellphone and charger, blankets, sleeping bag, pillows, toiletries, extra clothes. What you won't need is a pre-made team, food, or drinks. We would really, really, really, appreciate it if you would not bring: Guns or other weapons, alcohol, illegal drugs, soldering or similar equipment, or animals.
  • How much will it cost me?
    • Food, drinks, swag, snacks, travel costs (within reason, details will be in the e-mail after you are accepted) and a place to sleep will all be provided to you for absolutely free.
  • Where/When will we be hacking?
    • At the moment exact location and scheduling is TBD. Once we find out you will be updated via e-mail!
  • What are the prizes?
    • Top prizes are TBA, but usually involve thousands of dollars and perks from company sponsors. Sponsors can also offer their own prizes for specific categories, which they judge and fund themselves.
  • Who is running this thing?
    • Owlhacks is run by a group of insane, sleep-deprieved students from Temple University's TUDevs club. All funding for Owlhacks comes from corporate sponsor donations, and some on the side panhandling in Center City.
