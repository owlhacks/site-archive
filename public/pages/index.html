Our FAQ Answers to OWL of your questions
When is Owlhacks?
Owlhacks will begin on the evening of Friday, April 17th at 8pm and will end on April 19th at 4pm.
Who can participate in Owlhacks?
All the most awesome student developers under the sun! Owlhacks is open for any student to attend - beginners included. If you're a student, make sure you register.
How do I get in?
The first step to getting into Owlhacks is applying. When you register, you submit a bunch of information about yourself that helps us decide if you'd be a good fit for Owlhacks. After we make a decision, we'll send you an email with the results.
Why come to Owlhacks?
Owlhacks is an opportunity to grow as a creator, not just a developer. You'll be surrounded by some of the most creative and analytical minds in the country. Some of the most cutting-edge technologies will be on display, and by the end of the weekend, you could even win an award.
Do Temple University students have to apply?
Yes! Temple students should register using the form along with everybody else.
How do I get involved if I'm not a student?
Only students can be hackers, but advisors are always handy. Shoot us an email if you're interested.
How much does Owlhacks cost?
Nothing! You don't need money to come to Owlhacks, just the willingness to learn and enthusiasm to create. You'll get free food and swag throughout the weekend - all you have to do is show up.
Can High School students participate?
All we require from our applicants is a .edu email address (for Canadians or others whose schools don't have .edu emails, just give us your school email). If you're in high school, and you have an appropriate email address, then you can apply to come to Owlhacks.
Who do I talk to if I have any questions?
If you have any questions or comments at all, don't hesitate to send an email to team@owlhacks.com.
How big can teams be?
We allow up to 4 people per team.
